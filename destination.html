Travelogue: Richmond, Virgina

Overview:

Richmond, Virginia's capital city, is one of the oldest major cities in the United States. The primary drivers of Richmond's economy are law, finance, and government. Various areas in the city contain many government agencies and Fortune 500 companies.

Must-See Spots:

  1. Virginia Holocaust Museum
  2. Maymont Park
  3. The Poe Museum
  4. Virginia State Capitol
  5. The Byrd Theatre

Visitor Tips:

  • The GRTC (Greater Richmond Transit Company) is a free bus system.
  • Richmond is actively becoming more bike-friendly, as there are dedicated bike paths.
  • Expect hot, humid weather during the summer; it'll be around 85 degrees Fahrenheit on average.
  • If you like beer, visit some of the local breweries, as they're one of the main attractions that the city is known for.
